<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>

This project analyzes over 11,000 Diwali sales transactions using Python to understand customer purchasing behavior and sales trends. It identifies key customer segments, top-performing regions, and popular product categories to support data-driven business decisions.

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-problem"></a>Business Problem</h2>

Retail businesses often struggle to identify:

- Which customer segments contribute the most revenue.
- Which states generate the highest sales.
- Which product categories are most profitable.
- How customer demographics influence purchasing behavior.

This project addresses these challenges by analyzing historical sales data to support data-driven marketing and business decisions.

<h2><a class="anchor" id="key-insights"></a>Key Insights</h2>

- Female customers contributed the highest overall sales.
- Customers aged 26–35 years were the largest and highest-spending demographic.
- Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Delhi generated the highest sales and order volumes.
- Professionals from the IT, Healthcare, Aviation, and Banking sectors recorded the highest spending.
- Food, Clothing & Apparel, Electronics & Gadgets, and Footwear & Shoes were the top-performing product categories.
- The Top 10 products accounted for a significant share of total customer orders.

<h2><a class="anchor" id="final-recommendations"></a>Final Recommendations</h2>

- Focus marketing campaigns on female customers, who contribute the highest share of sales.
- Develop personalized promotions for customers aged 26–35, the most valuable customer segment.
- Increase inventory and promotional efforts in Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Delhi to maximize festive sales.
- Target professionals in IT, Healthcare, Aviation, and Banking with tailored offers and loyalty programs.
- Prioritize inventory for high-performing categories such as Food, Clothing & Apparel, Electronics & Gadgets, and Footwear & Shoes during festive seasons.
- Implement customer segmentation based on demographics and purchase history to deliver personalized offers and improve customer retention.
